Welcome to Blood Bros by the Mallocs.

We are a group of studients from UPC, studying a Design and Development degree and this is the website where we will be uploading our release versions, Blood Bros is a shooter arcade game based on the far west, originaly made by TAD Corporation. The main characters are a cowboy and a Native American, which are two brothers with the goal to capture the most dangerous bandit of the West, Big Bad John
